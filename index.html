Brief About Us

The Society of Mechanical and Automotive Engineers (SMAE) is a prestigious professional organization dedicated to promoting excellence in the fields of mechanical engineering and automotive technology.

SMAE brings together a diverse community of engineers, researchers, academics, and industry professionals who are passionate about advancing the knowledge and practice of mechanical and automotive engineering.

We are a 9-year young technology company on a mission to equip students with relevant skills & practical exposure through internships, trainings, and competitions.
